Three hundred years after the establishment of the first colony on the Moon, humankind is ready to get to the next frontier: Mars.

However, the new Mars Landing rocket is very expensive, and it will require a huge amount of the ultra-rare space mineral Iridium to be funded.

Use the arrow keys to help Major Tom travel through space in search of Iridium rocks. Each rocket part has a cost of $1,000,000. If this amount is collected, it will be inmediately invested in the construction of the rocket.

Avoid the meteorites!
